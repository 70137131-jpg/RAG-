# Claude-Inspired RAG Frontend Design Tokens

## Colors
- **Background (Main, Sidebar)**: `#1A1A1A`
- **Background (Composer, hovered items)**: `#2C2C2C`
- **Text (Primary)**: `#EDEDED`
- **Text (Secondary / Muted)**: `#9B9B9B`
- **Accent (Send Button)**: `#D97757`
- **Accent Hover (Send Button Hover)**: `#C1684B`
- **Lines / Borders**: `#3A3A3A`

## Typography
- **Font Stack**: `'Inter', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif`
- **Weights**: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semibold)
- **Sizes**:
  - Base: `15px`
  - Small: `13px`
  - Header: `16px`

## Spacing & Radius
- **Border Radius (Composer, Buttons)**: `12px`
- **Border Radius (Sidebar Items)**: `8px`
- **Padding (Composer)**: `12px 16px`
- **Padding (Messages)**: `24px`

## Motion
- **Message Appearance**: Simple 200ms fade-in, no sliding or scaling.
